About

  • We are the QMUL AI and Robotics Society and have been representing the university through events with our world renouned researchers.
  • We have competitions such as our very own Robot Wars.
  • We also hold tutorials where we help our members dive into the world of technology.

  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our society was started by a group of EECS and SEMS students who were passionate about Robotics and wanted to have a place to share that interest with the world.

  • November 2016

    Robotics 101 Sessions start

    We hosted our first round of successful Robotics 101 sessions

  • December 2017

    States of Flux Soft Robotics Talk held

    Hosted a conference on Soft Robotics Alongside the Advanced Robotics Centre at QMUL

  • March 2019

    First Robot Wars Tournament

    The first internal Robot Wars was held within the Robotics Society
